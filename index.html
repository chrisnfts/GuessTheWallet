How to use ?

Crypto wallets contain a passphrase of 12 words (such as coinbase or metamask), and these words are taken from (bip39 word list), which is a list of 2048 words.

This site randomly searches for passphrase for wallet based on (bip39 word list). This passphrase can be expected, (especially with the increase in the number of wallets), but The number of possibilities is very high although the chance of finding a passphrase for a wallet is very small, but who knows maybe you will find a wallet with a lot of money.
